About Catalyst

Catalyst is a first-person shooter game with a monetization system that includes free and purchasable loot boxes containing skins. The gameplay features four damage zones, bleeding, health, and body armor. Players share money, and aiming while moving affects gun recoil. The game features various weapons and utilities, with each having unique stats and costs.

Gameplay

Catalyst's gameplay is challenging and exciting, with four damage zones, bleeding, health, and body armor. Players share money, and aiming while moving affects gun recoil, making accuracy and strategy crucial to winning. With various weapons and utilities, each with unique stats and costs, you can customize your gameplay experience to suit your style.

Gamemodes

Catalyst has different gamemodes to choose from, including Unranked, Ranked, Retakes, Custom, Deathmatch, Wingman, and 1v1. Ranked has a skill-based matchmaking system that ensures fair matches, while the other modes offer a variety of gameplay styles.

Maps

Catalyst features five different maps: Sandstorm, Sakura, Street, Shoreside, and Cargo. Each map offers unique challenges and strategies, so you'll always have something new to experience.
